Yesterday I was doing my daily ~~doomscroll~~ traveling across Mastodon when I stumbled across this blog called [just put it on your blog](https://shellsharks.com/just-put-it-on-your-blog). Is a short post that you should go and read, but in summary the author talks about that you should put in a blog the things that you want to share, it doesn't matter what it is: a note, random thought, a strange dream, something you like or hate a lot, or even a recipe. I doesn't have to be something scencially special or written in the most elegant way, just something you wanna share.

After reading it I was inspired, is the reason why after hesitating for long time (and explore that website for... long enough) I finally decided to create this blog.

Here I'll be posting things like technical resources I find about programming, linux and servers, music, things I do in minecraft or other video games, some random though that I can't get out of my head, maybe a review? and for sure links to other nice websites.
